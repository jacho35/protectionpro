How each result is verified

Two kinds of anchor are used. Where a standard prescribes a formula, the engine output is pinned to that formula evaluated by hand (IEC 60909, IEEE 1584, IEEE 80). Where the physics has a closed-form solution, the engine is pinned to first principles (equal-area criterion for critical clearing time; the maximum-power-transfer nose for voltage collapse). Each row below is an executable assertion in the backend regression suite — a deviation outside tolerance fails the build.
